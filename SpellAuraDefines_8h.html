◆ AuraType

enum AuraType

This is what's used in a Modifier by the Aura class to tell what the Aura should modify.

Enumerator
SPELL_AURA_NONE 
SPELL_AURA_BIND_SIGHT 
SPELL_AURA_MOD_POSSESS 
SPELL_AURA_PERIODIC_DAMAGE 

The aura should do periodic damage, the function that handles this is Aura::HandlePeriodicDamage, the amount is usually decided by the Unit::SpellDamageBonusDone or Unit::MeleeDamageBonusDone which increases/decreases the Modifier::m_amount.

SPELL_AURA_DUMMY 

Used by Aura::HandleAuraDummy.

SPELL_AURA_MOD_CONFUSE 

Used by Aura::HandleModConfuse, will either confuse or unconfuse the target depending on whether the apply flag is set.

SPELL_AURA_MOD_CHARM 
SPELL_AURA_MOD_FEAR 
SPELL_AURA_PERIODIC_HEAL 

The aura will do periodic heals of a target, handled by Aura::HandlePeriodicHeal, uses Unit::SpellHealingBonusDone to calculate whether to increase or decrease Modifier::m_amount.

SPELL_AURA_MOD_ATTACKSPEED 

Changes the attackspeed, the Modifier::m_amount decides how much we change in percent, ie, if the m_amount is 50 the attackspeed will increase by 50%.

SPELL_AURA_MOD_THREAT 

Modifies the threat that the Aura does in percent, the Modifier::m_miscvalue decides which of the SpellSchools it should affect threat for.

See also
SpellSchoolMask
SPELL_AURA_MOD_TAUNT 

Just applies a taunt which will change the threat a mob has Taken care of in Aura::HandleModThreat.

SPELL_AURA_MOD_STUN 

Stuns targets in different ways, taken care of in Aura::HandleAuraModStun.

SPELL_AURA_MOD_DAMAGE_DONE 

Changes the damage done by a weapon in any hand, the Modifier::m_miscvalue will tell what school the damage is from, it's used as a bitmask.

See also
SpellSchoolMask
SPELL_AURA_MOD_DAMAGE_TAKEN 

Not handled by the Aura class but instead this is implemented in Unit::MeleeDamageBonusTaken and Unit::SpellBaseDamageBonusTaken.

SPELL_AURA_DAMAGE_SHIELD 

Not handled by the Aura class, implemented in Unit::DealMeleeDamage.

SPELL_AURA_MOD_STEALTH 

Taken care of in Aura::HandleModStealth, take note that this is not the same thing as invisibility.

SPELL_AURA_MOD_STEALTH_DETECT 

Not handled by the Aura class, implemented in Unit::isVisibleForOrDetect which does a lot of checks to determine whether the person is visible or not, the SPELL_AURA_MOD_STEALTH seems to determine how in/visible ie a rogue is.

SPELL_AURA_MOD_INVISIBILITY 

Handled by Aura::HandleInvisibility, the Modifier::m_miscvalue in the struct seems to decide what kind of invisibility it is with a bitflag.

the miscvalue decides which bit is set, ie: 3 would make the 3rd bit be set.

SPELL_AURA_MOD_INVISIBILITY_DETECTION 

Adds one of the kinds of detections to the possible detections.

As in SPEALL_AURA_MOD_INVISIBILITY the Modifier::m_miscvalue seems to decide what kind of invisibility the Unit should be able to detect.

SPELL_AURA_OBS_MOD_HEALTH 
SPELL_AURA_OBS_MOD_MANA 
SPELL_AURA_MOD_RESISTANCE 

Handled by Aura::HandleAuraModResistance, changes the resistance for a unit the field Modifier::m_miscvalue decides which kind of resistance that should be changed, for possible values see SpellSchools.

See also
SpellSchools
SPELL_AURA_PERIODIC_TRIGGER_SPELL 

Currently just sets Aura::m_isPeriodic to apply and has a special case for Curse of the Plaguebringer.

SPELL_AURA_PERIODIC_ENERGIZE 

Just sets Aura::m_isPeriodic to apply.

SPELL_AURA_MOD_PACIFY 

Changes whether the target is pacified or not depending on the apply flag.

Pacify makes the target silenced and have all it's attack skill disabled. See: http://classic.wowhead.com/spell=6462

SPELL_AURA_MOD_ROOT 

Roots or unroots the target.

SPELL_AURA_MOD_SILENCE 

Silences the target and stops and spell casts that should be stopped, they have the flag SpellPreventionType::SPELL_PREVENTION_TYPE_SILENCE.
